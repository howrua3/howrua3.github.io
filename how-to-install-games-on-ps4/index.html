The Pain Points of Installing Games on PS4

There are a few pain points that come with installing games on PS4. First off, you need to make sure that you have enough space on your hard drive to accommodate the game. Additionally, if you’re not connected to the internet, you may have trouble accessing all of the features of the game.

The process of installing games on PS4 is actually pretty straightforward. Here’s what you need to do:

  2. Go to your PS4 home screen and select the game you want to install
  3. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation process

How to Free Up Space on Your Hard Drive
