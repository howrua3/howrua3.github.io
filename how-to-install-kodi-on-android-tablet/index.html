How to Install Kodi on Android Tablet

Follow these simple steps to download and install Kodi on your Android tablet:

  1. Go to the Google Play Store on your Android tablet.
  2. In the search bar, type “Kodi” and press enter.
  3. Select the official Kodi app from the search results.
  4. Click “Install” and wait for the app to download and install on your tablet.
  5. Once installed, open the Kodi app and start streaming your favorite media!

Q: What is an add-on?

A: An add-on is a piece of software that adds additional functionality to Kodi. Add-ons can be used to access additional media content, change the interface, and more.

Q: Do I need to root my Android tablet to install Kodi?

A: No, you do not need to root your Android tablet to install Kodi. Simply download the app from the Google Play Store and start streaming!
