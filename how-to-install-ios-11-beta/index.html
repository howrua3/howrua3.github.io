How to Install iOS 11 Beta?

The installation of iOS 11 beta on your iPhone or iPad isn’t too complicated. Provided you follow the instructions carefully and have access to a secure internet connection, you can install the latest iOS updates with ease.

In this guide, we’ll take you through the installation process step-by-step:

Prerequisites

You need to take care of the following requirements before the installation process:

  • First, create a complete backup of your iPhone or iPad to prevent the loss of data.
  • Ensure a secure and stable internet connection.
  • Make sure your device is compatible with iOS 11

Once you ’re sure that you’ve met all these prerequisites, you can proceed to the installation process. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Download the iOS 11 Beta Profile

The first step to install iOS 11 beta is to download the iOS 11 beta profile onto your device. It helps you access iOS 11 beta updates and is relatively easy to download. You can find the iOS 11 beta profile on websites such as BetaProfiles. Here’s how you can download and install the profile on your device:

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone or iPad device and browse for iOS 11 beta profiles. We recommend visiting the BetaProfiles website to download the latest version of the iOS 11 beta profile.
  2. Select the ‘Download’ option for the iOS 11 beta profile.
  3. Choose your iPhone or iPad device when prompted to install the profile.
  4. Enter your device passcode when prompted.
  5. Review and agree to the terms and conditions.
  6. Restart your device when prompted.

Step 2: Download and Install iOS 11 Beta

After downloading and installing the iOS 11 beta profile, the next step is to download and install the iOS 11 beta update on your device. Here’s how you can do it:

  1. Open ‘Settings’ on your device.
  2. Tap on ‘General.’
  3. Select ‘Software Update.’
  4. Tap the ‘Download and Install’ button.
  5. Enter your device passcode when prompted and wait for the installation process to complete.
  6. Restart your device when prompted.

Step 3: Verify iOS 11 Beta Installation

After completing the installation process, you should verify whether iOS 11 beta has been installed successfully or not. Here’s how you can verify it:

  1. Open ‘Settings’ on your device.
  2. Tap on ‘General.’
  3. Select ‘About.’
  4. Check the software version, and if it’s 11, congratulations, you have successfully installed iOS 11 beta on your device!

Question and Answer

Q. Can I install the iOS 11 beta on any iPhone or iPad?

A. No, not all iPhone or iPad devices are compatible with iOS 11 beta. You can check if your device is eligible by visiting the Apple website or contacting customer support.

Q. Can I install iOS 11 beta without a developer account?

A. Yes, you can install iOS 11 beta without a developer account by downloading the iOS 11 beta profile and following the installation steps mentioned above.

Q. Will I lose my data while installing iOS 11 beta?

A. No, but it’s still recommended to create a backup of your device before installing iOS 11 beta to ensure the safety of your data.

Q. Can I switch back to the previous iOS version after installing iOS 11 beta?

A. Yes, you can go back to the previous iOS version by uninstalling the iOS 11 beta profile and restoring your device to factory settings.
