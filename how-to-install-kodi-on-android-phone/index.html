Many Android users face the challenge of installing new apps on their phone, which can be a bit confusing and intimidating, especially for those who are not familiar with technology. The Solution: How to Install Kodi on Android Phone

Before we start the installation process, note that Kodi is not available on the Google Play Store, so you need to download the APK file from a third-party website.

Step 1: Go to your phone settings, then to security and enable installations from unknown sources.

Step 2: Download the Kodi APK file from the official website or any other reliable source.

Step 3: Once the APK file is downloaded, tap on it and click “install.”

Step 4: After the installation is complete, you can click on the Kodi icon and enjoy your favorite shows and movies.
