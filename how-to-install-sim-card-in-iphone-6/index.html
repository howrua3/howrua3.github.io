How to Install SIM Card on iPhone 6

First, locate the SIM card tray on the right edge of your iPhone 6. You should see a small hole next to the tray. Find a paperclip or SIM eject tool and insert it into the hole. This will release the tray and allow you to remove it.

Next, insert your SIM card into the tray. Make sure it’s positioned correctly and push the tray back into the phone until it clicks into place.

Once you’ve inserted your SIM card and replaced the tray, you’re all set. Give your iPhone a few moments to recognize and activate your SIM card, and you should be good to go.
