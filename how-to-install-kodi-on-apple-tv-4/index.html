How to Install Kodi on Apple TV 4

First, you’ll need a Mac computer with Xcode 9 or later installed. Once you have Xcode set up, follow these steps:

Step 1: Download Cydia Impactor and Kodi 17.3 IPA file onto your Mac

To install Kodi, you’ll need to download a couple of files first. Firstly, download Cydia Impactor and keep it ready for use. Next, download the latest Kodi 17.3 IPA file. These files are readily available on various websites, but make sure to download from a trusted source.

Step 2: Connect your Apple TV 4 to your Mac using a USB-C cable

Using your USB-C cable, connect your Apple TV 4 to your Mac computer. Then, launch the latest version of Xcode and go to the ‘Window’ option on the menu bar. From there, select ‘Devices and Simulators’ option.

Step 3: Trust your Mac computer on your Apple TV 4

On your Apple TV, go to the ‘Settings’ option and then to ‘Remotes and Devices.’ From there, select ‘Bluetooth’ and then your Mac computer’s name. A four-digit code will appear on your Apple TV, which you’ll need to type in on your Mac computer to complete the pairing process.

Step 4: Launch Cydia Impactor on your Mac

Once you’ve successfully paired your devices, launch Cydia Impactor on your Mac.

Step 5: Drag and drop the Kodi file onto Cydia Impactor

Next, drag and drop the Kodi IPA file onto Cydia Impactor. You’ll be prompted to enter your Apple ID and password.

Step 6: Trust the developer profile

After entering your Apple ID and password, Cydia Impactor will install the Kodi application on your Apple TV 4. Once the installation is complete, you’ll see the Kodi app on your Apple TV’s home screen. But before you can use the app, you’ll need to go to ‘Settings,’ then ‘General,’ then ‘Profiles & Device Management.’ From there, you need to trust the developer profile for Kodi.

Step 7: Enjoy Kodi on your Apple TV 4!

And that’s it! Now you can enjoy all the features and add-ons that Kodi has to offer on your Apple TV 4.
