Answering the Target of Installing Exodus on Kodi 17

The good news is that installing Exodus on Kodi 17 is not as difficult as it may seem. The first step is to ensure that you have Kodi 17 installed on your device. Once that is done, you can follow these simple steps:

  1. Launch Kodi 17 and click on the “Settings” gear icon.
  2. Choose “System Settings” from the menu.
  3. Select “Add-ons” and then enable “Unknown Sources.”
  4. Click “Yes” when the warning message appears.
  5. Go back to the settings menu and select “File Manager.”
  6. Click on “Add Source” and then choose “None.”
  7. Enter the URL for the repository where Exodus is located. (You can find this URL from various online sources).
  8. Give the source a name.
  9. Go back to the Kodi home screen and select “Add-ons.”
  10. Click on the “Package Installer” icon.
  11. Select “Install from zip file.”
  12. Find the repository you added earlier and select it.
  13. Click on the Exodus zip file and wait for the installation to complete.
  14. Return to the Kodi home screen and click on “Add-ons.”
  15. Exodus will now be listed, and you can start watching your favorite shows!
